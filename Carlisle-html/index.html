SERVICES WE OFFER

Our Resorts provide spacious suites for your pet with comfortable bedding, pleasing amenities, and lots of love!
Your loved one will be pampered with our attention to details.

Lots of Breaks!

Your dog will get at least three potty breaks and plenty of playtime, which is included! Our furry friends are always supervised, and our animal loving staff makes sure this is a fun experience!

Pampering Your Pet

If your pet is a picky eater. Not a problem. We'll take time to get your pet acclimated to his new environment. Keeping your pet healthy while you're on vacation is our focus. We'll even hand feed your pet to ensure his nutrition.

Spa Services

You can schedule a spa day for your pet with bath, mini-groom or full-groom at Milo's Spa. We highly recommend exit baths prior to departure so your pet is fresh for your arrival.


We've got treats!

Order a delicious treat for your pet during his or her stay. We have plenty of homemade treats to choose from, including our doggie favorite... The peanut butter filled hoof!



Spotless Suites

We clean each suite every day, three times a day, and that includes mopping the floor. Our floors are finished as opposed to exposed concrete which may trap odors and bacteria, ensuring a safe and clean environment.
